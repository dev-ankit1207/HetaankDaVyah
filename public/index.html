Our love story

First meet

Feb 14 2020

We met for the first time on valentine's day. Yes, it was a valentine's day but no, we were not dating yet!!
It was just a friendly meet and who knew that meet would change our lives. Though we were little nervous as we were meeting alone for the first tym but it was the most memorable and funny day. Firstly, as it was a valentine's day I was very scared that Ankit might propose me but,he didn't so it was cool. The most funny thing about that meet was that Hetvi was so lost in thoughts that Ankit came to meet her between his working hours and again going to office she accidentally took a wrong fast train and instead of borivali she reached bandra 🤣
Apart from this many more funny events took place and we connected so easily as if we know each other from years.

First date

May 16 2020

Ahh!! What to say about our first date. The date which never happened and ofcourse all thanks to Covid-19. But, technology is the best thing right? So, we ended up having a virtual movie date!! Trust me, though we weren't together to feel each other's presence but we enjoyed alot. We made most out of it as much as we could have done. It was a perfect virtual movie date night.

Proposal

Sept 07 2020

You know when everything around you suddenly falls into place, and you feel like sharing all of your good and bad moments with someone special, and you want to tell that person your inner feelings, but you don't have the courage to do so, so your friends, her sisters all encouraged you to propose to her, and you finally propose to her on her birthday, which was the best gift to her and to myself.

Enagagement

June 25 2022

Suddenly things changes, till now the decision for your life were taken only for you, now you have to think for both, there were many opposite things between us but we started accepting those things, getting happy in small things, and then after unlimited fights, Virtual dates, Unlimited Masti, accepting a Long Distance relationship, trusting was the key part of LDR, and yes WE TOGETHER pass this test and we finally got engaged and started our journey to forever.
